   * Impact: Reduces the time for a surveyor to visit and improves customer trust with instant feedback.

   * The Feature: Analyze the CommunicationLog timestamps. The AI can predict which leads are most likely to pick up the phone at

   * Impact: Drastically improves agent "Connect Rates" and reduces wasted calling effort.

  4. Real-time Agent "Co-Pilot" (Sales Coaching)

   * Impact: Turns junior agents into top-performers by giving them expert knowledge in real-time.

  5. Automated Underwriting (Risk Profiling)
   * The Feature: Instead of just asking 5 questions, the AI can cross-reference the user's answers. If a user says they are a "Skydiver"
     but want "Standard Life Insurance," the AI flags it for Manual Review or automatically adjusts the premium_base in the PolicyModel
     for that specific quote.
   * Impact: More accurate pricing and lower risk for the insurance provider.

  6. Voice-to-Dashboard (Management AI)
   * The Feature: Add a "Command Bar" for the Admin. Instead of clicking through filters, the admin can type (or say): "Show me all
     Health Insurance leads from Mumbai who haven't been contacted in 3 days."
   * Impact: Zero-learning curve for managers to get complex data insights.

  7. AI Sentiment-Based Churn Alert
   * The Feature: The AI monitors the Message table. If it detects words like "disappointed," "switch," "expensive," or "cancel," it
     automatically moves that customer to a "High Risk Churn" category and alerts a senior retention agent.
   * Impact: Saves revenue by catching unhappy customers before they leave.

  8. Competitor Price Comparison Engine
   * The Feature: The AI can look at the selected policy in your database and search/compare it against live market data to show a: "Why
     this plan is better" card (e.g., "20% cheaper than Market Average" or "Covers 5 more critical illnesses than Plan X").

✦ To provide maximum business value and differentiate your platform from competitors, here are high-ROI, strategic AI features that focus
  on Revenue Growth, Compliance, and Fraud Prevention:

  1. Automated Compliance & Quality Audit (The "Safety Net")
   * The Value: In insurance, "mis-selling" (agents making false promises) leads to massive fines.
   * The Feature: An AI agent that scans 100% of the Message and CommunicationLog records. It flags any conversation where an agent uses
     non-compliant language (e.g., promising "guaranteed 100% returns" on a risky product or skipping the mandatory "Terms and
     Conditions" disclaimer).
   * Tech Hint: Use an LLM with a specific "Compliance Rubric" to score every chat.

  2. Propensity-to-Buy Modeling (Cross-Sell Engine)
   * The Value: It’s 5x cheaper to sell to an existing customer than to find a new one.
   * The Feature: Analyze your CustomerPolicy table. If a customer has "Motor Insurance" and their created_at date suggests they just
     bought a new car, the AI predicts they might soon need "Travel Insurance" (vacation) or "Life Insurance" (asset protection).
   * The Action: The system automatically adds these customers to a "High Propensity" list in the payoutControllers.js logic for targeted
     campaigns.

  3. Claims Fraud Network Analysis
   * The Value: Fraud costs insurance companies billions.
   * The Feature: When a new Claim is submitted, the AI doesn't just look at that claim. it cross-references the Customer and Policy data
     to see if there are "Hidden Links"—e.g., three different customers from the same apartment building filed the same "Theft" claim
     within 30 days using the same doctor's note.
   * Impact: Stops organized fraud rings that traditional rule-based systems miss.

  4. AI-Powered "What-If" Coverage Simulator
   * The Value: Customers often don't buy because they don't really understand the benefit.
   * The Feature: An interactive tool on the Policy page. Instead of reading a table, the user types: "I have an accident and need 5 days
     of ICU in a Private Room in Delhi. How much will I pay out of pocket?" 
   * The Action: The AI calculates the "Room Rent Limit," "Copay," and "Deductibles" from the policy terms and gives a clear dollar/rupee
     figure.

  5. Automated "Video-KYC" Face Matching
   * The Value: Eliminates identity theft during the onboarding process.
   * The Feature: Use the Chatbot to ask the user to take a selfie and upload a photo of their ID. The AI performs Face Matching and
     Liveness Detection (ensuring it's not a photo of a photo) to verify the user instantly.
   * Impact: Moves the lead from "Warm" to "Verified" without a human ever checking the documents.

  6. Dynamic Renewal "Save" Strategies
   * The Value: Retention is the backbone of insurance.
   * The Feature: For policies in the CustomerPolicy table nearing expiry, the AI analyzes the customer's history. If they haven't made a
     claim, it automatically generates a "No-Claim Bonus (NCB) Appreciation" message. If they have made a claim and might see a price
     hike, it proactively suggests a different, more affordable plan to prevent them from leaving.

  7. Semantic Search for Agents (Internal Knowledge Base)
   * The Value: Agents spend 20% of their time looking for policy details in long PDFs.
   * The Feature: Convert all your Policy Wordings (PDFs) into "Embeddings." An agent can type into their dashboard: "Does the Star
     Health Optima plan cover robotic surgeries?" and get the exact paragraph and page number instantly.
   * Impact: Reduces "Hold Time" during calls and increases agent confidence.
